Two of our most-asked-about programmes, side by side.

You mentioned on our call that you were weighing IGNOU against Symbiosis. Here is the breakdown the way I would have walked you through it, in writing this time, so you can revisit it before you decide.

The headline: IGNOU costs about a sixth of Symbiosis, but they serve different audiences. Neither is "better." The right one depends on what role you are aiming for in two years.

If your goal is a government promotion or PSU eligibility, IGNOU is genuinely the strong choice. If it is a corporate management role at a brand-conscious employer, Symbiosis usually wins.

Other things to weigh:

Time on the calendar: IGNOU is 2.5 years, Symbiosis is 2. If you are 33+, the six months matters.
Specialisation depth: Symbiosis has fewer streams but deeper electives in HR and Marketing.
Payment flexibility: Both offer EMI. Symbiosis EMI tends to be more structured for working professionals.
